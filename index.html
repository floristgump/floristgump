FAQ

Q: Are these flowers real?
Yes, these flowers are real. These flowers are a type of chrysanthemum that was process using frosting technology to sustain the softness and moisture of the flowers. The flowers are fully imported from Japan.

Q: How frequent should I water the flowers?
Snowy Flowers are recommend to be sprayed only 3-5 times a week.

Q: Will the flowers grow longer or grow more?
No. Snowy Flowers will not grow longer or grow more. Snowy Flowers has been frozen as it will not further grow.

Q: Do I need to place the flowers under the sun?
No. Snowy Flowers are indoor plants. Snowy Flowers does not require direct sunlight.
